hyPACK-2013 Accommodation


Limited accommodation facility for four days stay to attend workshop is arranged in LakeView Guest House, University of Hyderabad (UoH), Tagore International House (TIH), UoH. Also, accommodation is arranged in Private Service apartments and Hotel. The hyPACK-2013 Registration fee does not include accommodation charges and will have to be borne separately by the participant.

The Lakeview Guesthouse is 2 km away from main entrance of UOH campus.

The Tagore International House (TIH) is located at South-campus of UoH and it 3.5 km away from main entrance of UoH Campus.

The hyPACK-2013 Venue i.e. CMSD is 2.5 Km away from Lakeview Guest House & 4 km away from TIH. Pick-up & drop is arranged from Lakeview Guesthouse to venue (CMSD) & back, TIH to venue (CMSD) & back as well as from Pvt Service Aprt. to venue (CMSD) & back.
