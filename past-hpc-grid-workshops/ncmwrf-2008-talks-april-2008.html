Parallel Computing workshop Technical Programme - Programming Paradigms - CAM
(High Performance Data Mining; Computational Algorithms for CAM; MPI 2.0)
Dr.VCV.Rao, C-DAC, Pune University Campus

The HPC Solution Group, C-DAC, Pune organised this event at NCMWRF, Noida in collaboration with Betatesting Group, C-DAC, Pune and other technical staff members. HPC Solution Group members and Dr. V.Sundararajan, Group Coordinator, Scientific and Engineering Computing Group delivered talks on f90, and OpenMP on April 10-12, and April 15, 2008.
